## How the Affirmations Work

The web app automatically displays the affirmation that corresponds to your current progress:

- **Starting out?** You'll see Day 1's affirmation
- **Completed Day 5?** You'll see Day 6's affirmation (your next step)
- **Finished all 21 days?** You'll see the celebration affirmation from Day 21

Each affirmation is designed to:
- ✨ **Align with the day's tasks** - Reinforcing what you're working on
- 🎯 **Build confidence** - Affirming your capability and progress
- 💪 **Maintain momentum** - Keeping you motivated through the journey
- 🌟 **Celebrate milestones** - Acknowledging important transitions

The affirmation updates automatically as you complete days, creating a personalized experience that grows with your progress.

---

**Remember:** Start each day by reading your affirmation aloud and using the 1-minute timer to reflect on it deeply. This sets a positive, focused intention for your writing session.
