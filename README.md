# 21-Day Non-Fiction Book Writing Method - Interactive Web App

Transform your non-fiction book idea into a complete manuscript in just 21 days using this proven methodology.

## Features

### 🎯 Interactive Timeline
- **21-day structured journey** with clear phases: Foundation, Writing, Research & Polish, Clean Up, and Finalization
- Click on any day to view detailed tasks and track your progress
- Visual progress indicators show your completion status

### ✨ Daily Affirmations
- Built-in **1-minute affirmation timer**
- Daily affirmations to keep you motivated and focused
- "I am now a writer. I am an author. I am creative."

### ✅ Task Management
- Check off individual tasks as you complete them
- Automatic day completion tracking
- Celebration animations when you finish a day
- Progress persists using browser localStorage

### 📝 Note-Taking
- Add personal notes for each day
- Keep track of ideas, insights, and progress
- Notes are saved automatically to your browser

### 📊 Progress Tracking
- Real-time statistics showing your progress
- Completed days counter
- Percentage-based progress indicator

### 🎨 Premium Design
- Beautiful dark theme with vibrant gradients
- Smooth animations and micro-interactions
- Fully responsive design for all devices
- Glassmorphism effects and modern UI

## How to Use

1. **Open `index.html`** in your web browser
2. **Start with Day 1** - Click on "Day 1: Foundation" to begin
3. **Complete tasks** - Check off each task as you finish it
4. **Add notes** - Record your thoughts, ideas, and progress
5. **Track progress** - Watch your stats update as you complete days
6. **Use the timer** - Start the 1-minute affirmation timer each morning

## The Methodology

### Core Principles
- ⏱️ **Use Timers** - 10-minute tasks avoid perfectionism
- 🚀 **Keep Moving** - Don't polish as you write
- ✍️ **Write First, Edit Later** - Complete before worrying about quality
- 💬 **Write How You Talk** - Simple, clear, direct language
- 📊 **Know Your Market** - Publishers want moneymakers
- 🎯 **Structure First** - Outline completely before writing

### The Journey

**Week 1: Foundation & Writing**
- Days 1-2: Create your outline and structure
- Days 3-7: Start writing chapters

**Week 2: Writing & Research**
- Days 8-13: Continue writing chapters
- Day 14: Library research and fact-checking

**Week 3: Polish & Finalize**
- Days 15-16: First draft and read-through
- Days 17-20: Clean up and refinement
- Day 21: Final version creation

## Tips for Success

1. **Start each day with affirmations** - Use the 1-minute timer
2. **Don't skip ahead** - Follow the methodology in order
3. **Keep your spiral notebook handy** - Record ideas as they come
4. **Use the 10-minute timer** - Stay focused and avoid perfectionism
5. **Take the 3-day break** - After Day 21, rest before final edits
